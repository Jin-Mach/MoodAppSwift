# Mood App

A simple **iOS SwiftUI app** to track your daily mood, energy level, and optional notes.

## Features
- Record your mood (sad, neutral, happy)
- Track your energy level as a percentage
- Add short notes for each day
- View detailed history of past entries
- Easy-to-read interface with emojis and progress sliders

## Usage
1. Open the app on your iPhone.
2. Select your mood using the emoji buttons.
3. Adjust your energy level with the slider.
4. Optionally, add a short note.
5. Tap "Record" to save your entry.
6. View past entries in the History section.
